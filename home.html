Kirby's Adventures

This game takes you through 3 levels of varying/increasing difficulty. Don't worry, we have made sure that the levels are all beatable in a reasonable amount of time so rest assured. Follow kirby first throguh a doodle jump inspire map, reach a score of 100 to pass throught the first level. Then move onto the second level to to play a classic game of brick breaker except with kirby this time. And Finally follow Kirby to the final level of dragon quest where you have to keep track of may moving enemys and obstacles.
