Mega Man

This game takes you through the orginal Mega Man classic SNES stages. Don't worry, we have made sure that the levels are all beatable in a reasonable amount of time so rest assured. Follow Mega Man through the Bomb Man stage, reach a score by killing off enemies to pass throught the first level. Then move onto the second level to to play a classic game of more killing Mega Man npcs.
